The product

What is Subspace.money?

I found myself battling against badly designed bank apps, constantly wrestling with tax filings, and frustrations with investment tracking on spreadsheets. But, who said it has to be that way? So, I started working on Subspace.money! It's my solution to these day-to-day financial gripes, and I hope it can simplify your life too!

Why Subspace?

It works differently.

Most finance apps are built around categories and pie charts. Subspace is built around how money actually moves through your life — recurring, shared, goal-linked, and often invisible.

01

It tracks what banks ignore.

Banks log transactions. They don't tell you that your Netflix, Spotify, Amazon Prime, and Notion are quietly taking ₹3,200 a month. Subspace does.

02

Savings that follow your income.

Fixed goals break when life changes. Subspace adjusts your saving targets based on what came in this month — not what you planned six months ago.

03

Group money without the awkward texts.

Split a shared OTT plan, track who paid electricity, settle up — without a single message that says "hey, you still owe me."

Features

Everything your money needs.

Track Subscriptions

See every recurring charge in one place. Know exactly what renews, when it renews, and whether you're actually using it.

Split Bills

Create shared pools for any recurring expense. Members pay their share via UPI. No chasing. No awkward reminders.

Savings Goals

Set a target, pick a timeline. Subspace automatically adjusts monthly contributions based on what you earn and what you spend.

AI Analytics

Get proactive insights — not just charts. The AI flags anomalies, predicts next month's spend, and suggests where to cut.
